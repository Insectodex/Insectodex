Insectoplant Traits

Below you can find all possible traits for your Insectoplant characters, as well as the required features for each subspecies.

Each subspecies has their own trait sheet; some features will change rarities, appear, and disappear between the Insectoplant types!

All Insectoplants need a plant trait and spiracles!
